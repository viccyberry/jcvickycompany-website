About Us

JC Vicky Nig Ltd

We are a leading Chemical Company that provide a wide range of products for a variety of industries, we are primarily licensed to import and distribute industrial salts and chemicals such as Caustic soda, ethanol, Aluminum sulphate etc and chemicals for water treatment. Our chemicals are used to make beverages more refreshing, and steel stronger. We also have chemicals that are used in the production of soaps, detergent, and other everyday products.

Our Products

Food addictives: We offer a variety of food addictives that can be used to improve the taste, texture, and shelf life of food. Our addictives are safe for humans consumption and meet all applicable food safety regulations.

Food preservatives: We also offer a variety of food preservatives that can be used to prevent food from spoiling. Our preservatives are effective against a wide range of bacteria and mold, and they are safe for human consumption.

Chemicals for steel companies: We provide a range of chemicals that are used in the manufacture of steel. Our chemicals help to improve the strength, durability, and corrosion resistance of steel.

Chemicals for beverages: We offer a variety of chemicals that are used in the production of beverages. Our chemicals can be used to improve the taste, color, and fizziness of beverages.

Chemicals for oil and gas drilling: We provide a range of chemicals that are used in oil and gas drilling. Our chemicals help to lubricate the drill bit, prevent corrosion, and control the formation of scale and wax.

Chemicals for water treatment: We offer a range of chemicals that are used to treat water. Our chemicals can be used to remove impurities, disinfect water, and improve the taste and odor of water.

Chemicals for the production of soaps, detergent, and liquid soap: We provide a range of chemicals that are used in the production of soaps, detergents, and liquid soap. Our chemicals helps to clean surfaces, remove dirt and grime, and kill bacteria.

Our Commitment to safety:

We are committed to providing safe and high-quality chemicals to our custormers. We have strict quality control program in place to ensure that our products meet all applicable safety regulations. We also have a team of safety experts who are available to answer any question that you may have about our products.
